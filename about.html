Temple History and Significance

The Vitthal-Rakhumai Temple in ABC Village was founded over 50 years ago by devoted villagers. The temple has been the spiritual heart of our community, guiding generations toward peace and devotion.

Legend has it that the temple was established after a divine vision appeared to the village elders, directing them to build a shrine dedicated to Lord Vitthal and Goddess Rakhumai. Since then, the temple has been a beacon of faith and spirituality for the entire region.

Our Journey Through Time

1970

Foundation Laid

The temple foundation was laid by village elders after receiving divine guidance in their dreams.

1972

First Sanctum

The first sanctum was completed and the initial idols of Lord Vitthal and Goddess Rakhumai were installed.

1985

Expansion

The temple underwent its first major expansion to accommodate the growing number of devotees.

2005

Golden Jubilee

Celebrated 50 years of spiritual service to the community with special ceremonies and events.

2023

New Temple Project

Launched the initiative to build a new, larger temple to serve future generations.

About Lord Vitthal and Goddess Rakhumai

Lord Vitthal, also known as Vithoba, is a form of the Hindu god Vishnu. He is usually depicted as a dark young boy, standing on a brick, with his arms akimbo. Goddess Rakhumai (also known as Rukmini) is his consort, representing devotion and love.

The divine couple symbolizes the ideal relationship between the devotee and the divine, where the devotee (represented by Rakhumai) offers unconditional love and devotion to God (represented by Vitthal).

Many historians and researchers tried to find out the etymological origin of the word "Vitthal". Some scholars believe that it is a distorted form of the original word Vishnu. The words like Vitharas, Vitta found in various Kannad epigraphs are basically the elaboration of the word Vishnu.

The Great Saint poet Tukaram defines the word Vithoba in one of his abhangas that stands for 'Knowledge' + Thoba Stands for 'form' Thus Vithoba stands for the form of ultimate Knowledge or 'Idol of ultimate Knowledge'.

About ABC Village

Our Serene Community

ABC Village is a serene and vibrant community located in the heart of Maharashtra. Known for its unity and culture, the village celebrates Kartiki Ekadashi with great devotion every year.

The village is surrounded by lush green fields and is known for its warm, welcoming residents who take great pride in their cultural heritage and spiritual traditions.

Our village maintains a perfect balance between modern development and preservation of traditional values, making it an ideal place for spiritual growth and community living.

Cultural Heritage

ABC Village has a rich cultural heritage that dates back centuries. The community celebrates various festivals throughout the year with great enthusiasm and devotion.

Traditional arts, music, and dance forms are preserved and passed down through generations, keeping our cultural identity alive and vibrant.

The village is also known for its traditional crafts and culinary specialties that reflect the authentic Maharashtrian culture.
